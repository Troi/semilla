Semilla is a seed for an intentional community in Uruguay. It's not a prepper project – it's a hedge against demographic and fiscal collapse that works even if collapse doesn't come. A farm producing food, residency enabling legal stay, a community of people who see it the same way.

States fail slowly, then suddenly. Demographic decline, rising debt, falling trust in institutions. Uruguay is a geopolitical periphery with a climate enabling food self-sufficiency. The time window is now – between 2025 and 2035, before the anticipated recession.

I'm looking for people who bring more than money: doctors, agronomists, craftsmen, developers. People who understand that the best time to plant a tree was twenty years ago, the second best is now. If this makes sense, [write to me](mailto:vit@troi.cz).

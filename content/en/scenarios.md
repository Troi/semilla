## Conclusion

Semilla isn't the best strategy in BAU scenario – diversified portfolio or Czech real estate have better risk/reward. But Semilla is the only strategy that works well in **all** scenarios. That's the definition of a hedge.

If you believe BAU will continue forever, Semilla isn't for you. If you acknowledge the future is uncertain and want a strategy that works even when you're wrong, Semilla makes sense.
